Organization and Study Skills

Good writing is a product of careful planning, so I reinforce these steps in the writing process; however, some students also need overall assistance with keeping track of homework assignments and deadlines as well as budgeting time for various learning tasks. Depending on a student's needs, these areas can be integrated into our regular tutoring time or can be the main focus of our work together.
